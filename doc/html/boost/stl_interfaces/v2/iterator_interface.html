Description

A CRTP template that one may derive from to make defining iterators easier.

The template parameter D for iterator_interface may be an incomplete type. Before any member of the resulting specialization of iterator_interface other than special member functions is referenced, D shall be complete, and model std::derived_from<iterator_interface<D>>.
